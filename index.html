Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

We take the safety of our users seriously and strictly prohibit any content or activity that exploits or harms children.

  • We do not allow any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any sexually explicit content involving minors.
  • Any such content will be removed immediately and reported to the relevant authorities.
  • Users can report inappropriate content via the app's "Report" button.
  • We use both automated tools and manual review to moderate user-generated content.
